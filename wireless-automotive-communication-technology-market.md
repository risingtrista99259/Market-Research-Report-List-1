<p><p>Wireless Automotive Communication Technology enables vehicles to communicate with each other (V2V) and infrastructure (V2I) for enhanced safety and efficiency. **DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication)** is a standardized protocol using 5.9 GHz frequency for low-latency, high-reliability communication over short distances, ideal for real-time collision avoidance and traffic management. The **Mesh Market** involves interconnected networks where vehicles and infrastructure relay data dynamically, extending communication range and improving reliability in dense or complex environments. Both technologies aim to support autonomous driving and smart transportation systems.</p></p>
