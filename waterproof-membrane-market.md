<p><p>The waterproof membrane market includes several types, each with unique characteristics and applications. Modified Bitumen membranes are asphalt-based, offering excellent durability and flexibility. PVC membranes provide strong chemical resistance and are lightweight. EPDM membranes, made from synthetic rubber, excel in UV stability and elasticity. TPO membranes are energy-efficient and easy to install. HDPE membranes are known for their high-density properties and resistance to chemicals, while LDPE membranes are flexible and often used for various construction and agricultural applications.</p></p>
