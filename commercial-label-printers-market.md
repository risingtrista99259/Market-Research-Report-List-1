<p><p>The commercial label printer market is primarily categorized into three types: inkjet, laser, and others. Inkjet printers use liquid ink to produce high-quality images and are ideal for vibrant graphics on labels. Laser printers utilize a toner-based system, offering fast printing speeds and precision, making them suitable for high-volume label production. The "others" category encompasses thermal transfer and direct thermal printers, which are valued for their durability and low operating costs, catering to specific labeling needs in various industries.</p></p>
