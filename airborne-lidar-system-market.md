<p><p>The Airborne LiDAR System market is primarily divided into two types: Topographic LiDAR and Bathymetric LiDAR. Topographic LiDAR focuses on mapping and surveying terrestrial landscapes, capturing detailed elevation data for applications in forestry, urban planning, and infrastructure development. In contrast, Bathymetric LiDAR is designed for underwater terrain mapping, using laser pulses to measure seafloor depth and coastal features, crucial for marine navigation, habitat mapping, and environmental monitoring. Both types enhance data accuracy and efficiency in their respective fields.</p></p>
