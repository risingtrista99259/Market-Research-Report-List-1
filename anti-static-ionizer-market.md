<p><ul><li>Contact Type Ionizer</li><li>Induction Type Ionizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Static Ionizer market consists primarily of two types: Contact Type Ionizers and Induction Type Ionizers. Contact Type Ionizers utilize a direct connection to surfaces, effectively neutralizing static charges by delivering ions through direct contact. Induction Type Ionizers, on the other hand, generate ions at a distance, allowing for static neutralization without direct physical contact. Both types serve critical roles across industries like electronics, packaging, and textiles, ensuring product integrity and worker safety by mitigating static electricity-related issues.</p></p>
