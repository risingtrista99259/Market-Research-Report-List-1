<p><ul><li>Wet Friction Clutch</li><li>Dry Friction Clutch</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Automotive Clutch Market** includes three main types: **Wet Friction Clutch**, **Dry Friction Clutch**, and **Electromagnetic Clutch**. Wet friction clutches operate in an oil bath, offering smooth engagement and heat dissipation, ideal for high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. Dry friction clutches, common in manual transmissions, use dry surfaces for direct power transfer, providing efficiency and simplicity. Electromagnetic clutches use magnetic fields for engagement, enabling precise control and automation, often found in hybrid and electric vehicles for seamless power management. Each type caters to specific automotive needs.</p></p>
