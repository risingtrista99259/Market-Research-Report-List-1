<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet machine market is segmented into high-pressure and low-pressure systems. High-pressure waterjet machines, often exceeding 60,000 psi, are designed for precision cutting and are suitable for hard materials like metals and ceramics. They deliver rapid cutting speeds and intricate designs. Conversely, low-pressure systems typically operate below 20,000 psi and are used for softer materials, including plastics and textiles. These are ideal for applications requiring less precision and lower operational costs, making them accessible for various industries.</p></p>
