<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle ignition coil market includes two main types: single-spark and multi-spark coils. Single-spark coils generate one spark per ignition cycle, ensuring precise timing and reliable performance, commonly used in traditional engines. Multi-spark coils produce multiple sparks in rapid succession during a single cycle, enhancing combustion efficiency, reducing emissions, and improving fuel economy, ideal for modern, high-performance engines. Both types cater to varying engine technologies, with single-spark being cost-effective and multi-spark offering advanced performance benefits.</p></p>
