<p><p>The emulsion breaker market comprises two primary types: oil-soluble and water-soluble demulsifying agents. Oil-soluble demulsifiers are designed to disrupt oil-in-water emulsions, effectively separating oil from water phases, thereby enhancing oil recovery and refining processes. Conversely, water-soluble demulsifiers target water-in-oil emulsions, promoting the separation of water from oil. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including petroleum, chemical manufacturing, and wastewater treatment, optimizing processes by improving separation efficiency and minimizing operational challenges.</p></p>
