<p><p>The Narrowband Internet-of-Things (IoT) chipset market comprises three types: Stand-Alone, Guard Band, and In-Band. Stand-Alone chipsets operate independently, specifically designed for IoT applications without relying on existing networks. Guard Band chipsets utilize unused frequency bands to reduce interference, optimizing spectrum usage. In-Band chipsets, on the other hand, operate within existing communication channels, sharing bandwidth with other services. Each type serves distinct requirements for connectivity, efficiency, and spectrum management in the growing IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
