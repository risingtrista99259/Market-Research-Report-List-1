<p><p>The hard contact lens market includes gas permeable lenses, hybrid contact lenses, and PMMA lenses. Gas permeable lenses allow oxygen to pass through, promoting eye health while providing clear vision. Hybrid contact lenses combine the benefits of rigid gas permeable centers with soft edges for comfort. PMMA lenses, made from polymethyl methacrylate, were the first hard lenses, offering excellent vision but limited oxygen permeability. Each type caters to varying needs for comfort, vision correction, and eye health.</p></p>
