<p><p>The home wall heater market primarily consists of gas wall heaters and electric wall heaters. Gas wall heaters utilize natural gas or propane, offering efficient heating with quick response times and lower operating costs. They are often favored in regions with ample gas supply. Electric wall heaters, on the other hand, rely on electricity for operation, providing ease of installation and maintenance. They are suitable for areas without gas access and enable users to control heating in individual rooms effectively.</p></p>
