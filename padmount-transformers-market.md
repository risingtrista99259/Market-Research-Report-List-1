<p><p>The padmount transformers market is primarily divided into two types: dry-type and liquid-immersed padmount transformers. Dry-type padmount transformers utilize air for cooling and are known for their safety and low maintenance, making them ideal for urban substations and indoor applications. Conversely, liquid-immersed padmount transformers use oil for cooling, providing superior thermal management and efficiency, often suited for outdoor applications in utility networks. Both types serve critical roles in electrical distribution, catering to different operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
