<p><p>The underground loaders market is divided primarily into two types: underground internal combustion loaders and underground electric loaders. Internal combustion loaders are powered by diesel engines, offering robust performance and flexibility in challenging environments. They are typically favored for their high lifting capacity and mobility. Both types cater to different operational needs in mining and construction sectors.</p></p>
