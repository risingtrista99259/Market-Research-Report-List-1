<p><ul><li>Radiofrequency</li><li>Cryoablation</li><li>Laser</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Microwave</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac ablation technology encompasses various methods for treating arrhythmias by destroying problematic heart tissue. **Radiofrequency ablation** uses heat generated by high-frequency energy to eliminate unwanted electrical pathways. **Cryoablation** involves freezing tissue to achieve a similar effect. **Laser ablation** employs concentrated light to target and remove abnormal cells. **Ultrasound ablation** utilizes sound waves for tissue destruction. **Microwave ablation** applies microwave energy for targeted heating. Other techniques may include electrical stimulation or chemical approaches, broadening the treatment landscape.</p></p>
