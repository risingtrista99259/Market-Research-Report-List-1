<p><p>Cellular loudspeaker line arrays are categorized into two main types: active and passive. Active line arrays integrate built-in amplification and signal processing, allowing for better sound optimization and easier setup, making them ideal for larger venues. In contrast, passive line arrays require external amplifiers to drive the sound, offering flexibility for customization and typically being more cost-effective. Both types aim to enhance sound distribution, ensuring clarity and coverage in various acoustical environments.</p></p>
