<p><strong>The Soluble Coffee Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble coffee powder market is primarily segmented into two types: spray-drying and freeze-drying. Spray-drying involves rapidly drying liquid coffee extract using hot air, resulting in fine powder with a quick dissolution rate. This method is cost-effective and widely used. In contrast, freeze-drying preserves the coffee’s flavor and aroma by freezing the extract and removing moisture under vacuum conditions, producing a higher quality product. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and pricing strategies in the beverage industry.</p></p>
