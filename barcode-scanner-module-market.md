<p><p>The barcode scanner module market is divided into two main types: 1D and 2D barcode scanner modules. 1D barcode scanners read traditional linear barcodes, commonly found on products and packages, providing quick and straightforward data capture. Both modules see applications across industries like retail, logistics, and healthcare, driving efficiency in inventory management and data collection.</p></p>
