<p><p>The printing consumables market is primarily divided into two categories: inkjet consumables and laser consumables. Inkjet consumables include ink cartridges and printheads essential for inkjet printers, characterized by vibrant color output and versatility. Laser consumables, on the other hand, comprise toner cartridges and drum units used in laser printers, offering high-speed printing and sharp text quality. Both segments cater to distinct printing needs, with inkjet generally favored for images and laser optimal for high-volume document printing.</p></p>
