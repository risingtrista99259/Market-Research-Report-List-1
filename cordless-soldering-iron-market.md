<p><p>The cordless soldering iron market is primarily divided into two types: external and internal soldering irons. External soldering irons feature a replaceable heating element powered by batteries, providing flexibility and ease of use for various applications. In contrast, internal soldering irons integrate the heating element within the device, often designed for specific tasks with a compact, built-in battery system. Both types cater to different user preferences and requirements, supporting diverse industries and hobbyists in soldering tasks without the constraints of cords.</p></p>
