<p><p>The industrial 3D printers market encompasses various technologies that cater to different printing needs. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) uses thermoplastic filaments, while SLA (Stereolithography) employs liquid resin cured by UV light. SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) uses laser for powder fusion, and DMLS (Direct Metal Laser Sintering) specifically targets metal materials. 3DP (Three-Dimensional Printing) utilizes inkjet technology for layer creation. SLM (Selective Laser Melting) allows for high-density metal printing, and EBM (Electron Beam Melting) uses electron beams for melting materials, particularly in aerospace applications.</p></p>
