<p><p>The Foam Control Agent market includes two main types: De-foaming Agents and Anti-foaming Agents. De-foaming Agents are used to eliminate existing foam in industrial processes, ensuring smooth operations and preventing overflow. Anti-foaming Agents, on the other hand, are added to prevent foam formation during production or processing. Both types are essential in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals to maintain efficiency, product quality, and safety. They work by destabilizing foam bubbles or inhibiting their formation, ensuring optimal process performance.</p></p>
