<p><p>Horizontal carousels are automated storage and retrieval systems used to efficiently manage inventory in various industries. The market can be divided into hydraulic and mechanical types. Hydraulic horizontal carousels utilize fluid power to move shelves, offering smooth operation, higher load capacity, and quieter performance. In contrast, mechanical horizontal carousels rely on gears and motors, providing a cost-effective solution for lighter loads. Each type serves distinct operational needs and preferences, optimizing storage space and retrieval speed for businesses.</p></p>
