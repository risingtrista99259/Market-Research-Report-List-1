<p><p>The cardiology defibrillators market consists of several key types. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically placed devices that monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks as needed to correct life-threatening arrhythmias. External Defibrillators are portable devices used in emergencies to restore normal heart rhythm through shock delivery. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs) are non-invasive devices worn by patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest, providing continuous monitoring and shock delivery when necessary, thereby enhancing patient safety and management.</p></p>
