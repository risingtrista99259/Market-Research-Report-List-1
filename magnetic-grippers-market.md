<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Grippers</li><li>Permanent Magnet Grippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic grippers market is primarily divided into two types: electromagnetic grippers and permanent magnet grippers. Electromagnetic grippers utilize electrical energy to generate a magnetic field, allowing for adjustable gripping of ferromagnetic materials, ideal for applications requiring precision and adaptability. In contrast, permanent magnet grippers leverage permanent magnets for a consistent grip without the need for electricity, making them energy-efficient and reliable for handling heavy objects. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and automation, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
