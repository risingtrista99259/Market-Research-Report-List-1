<p><strong>The Flat Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat fiber cable market is categorized into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode fibers. Single-Mode cables feature a smaller core that allows only one light mode to propagate, ideal for long-distance communication due to lower attenuation. In contrast, Multi-Mode cables have a larger core, enabling multiple light modes to travel simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances with higher data rates. Both types cater to diverse telecommunications applications, influencing their adoption in various network infrastructures.</p></p>
