<p><p>A Rebar Upsetting Machine is an industrial device used to alter the diameter of rebar ends through a process known as upsetting. This technology enhances the bonding capability of rebars when they are welded or connected to other reinforcing elements in construction projects. The machine works by applying intense pressure to the ends of the rebar, resulting in a thicker or flared end that improves structural integrity and load-bearing capacity.</p><p>The Rebar Upsetting Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
