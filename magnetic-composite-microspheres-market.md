<p><p>The Magnetic Composite Microspheres market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Core-shell microspheres** consist of a magnetic core surrounded by a polymeric shell, enhancing stability and functionality. </p><p>2. **Anti-nucleoshell microspheres** feature a shell designed to repel nucleic acids, making them suitable for specific biomedical applications.</p><p>3. **Sandwich microspheres** incorporate multiple layers for improved magnetic properties and targeted delivery.</p><p>4. **Diffuse microspheres** are characterized by a homogeneous blend of materials, promoting uniform magnetic response for various applications.</p></p>
