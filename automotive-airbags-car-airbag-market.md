<p><p>The automotive airbag market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle safety. **Frontal Airbags** deploy from the steering wheel or dashboard to protect occupants in head-on collisions. **Side Airbags** are mounted in seats or doors, shielding against side impacts. **Curtain Airbags** extend along the roof to safeguard heads in rollovers or side crashes. **Knee Airbags** protect lower limbs during frontal collisions. **Others** include rear or inflatable seatbelt airbags, offering additional protection. Each type aims to minimize injury risks in specific crash scenarios.</p></p>
