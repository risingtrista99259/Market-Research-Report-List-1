<p><p>Bipolar Membrane Electrodialysis (BMED) is categorized into two primary market types: two-compartment and three-compartment systems. </p><p>In a two-compartment setup, the bipolar membrane separates two electrolyte solutions, allowing simultaneous ion separation and water splitting. In contrast, the three-compartment design incorporates an additional compartment, enhancing the separation efficiency and facilitating better product recovery. Both systems are utilized for applications such as desalination, acid-base recovery, and resource recovery, with each type offering specific benefits based on operational requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
