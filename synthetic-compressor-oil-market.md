PAO oils are widely used for their excellent lubricating properties and high thermal stability. PAG oils offer superior performance in extreme conditions, particularly in water-containing environments, while also providing better lubrication efficiency. Esters are derived from natural or synthetic sources, known for their biodegradability and lubrication effectiveness, making them suitable for eco-friendly applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
